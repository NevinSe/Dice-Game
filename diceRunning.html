Rules For the Game:
Roll a 20 sided die to see who attacks (if values match, reroll).
Roll a 6 sided die to determine Attack Value.
Roll a 4 sided die to determine Critical Hit multiplier.
Roll a 10 sided die to determine if attacked player can heal.
Roll a 8 sided die to find the value of health healed if succesful.
And finally, roll a 12 sided die to determine the value of gold stolen from attacked player.
Repeat until one player runs out of health.
